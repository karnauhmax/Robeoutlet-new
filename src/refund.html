Return and Refund policy

The return policy for all products on the site, complies with the Australia consumer protection Act. If you return goods due to a manufacturing fault, please take a photo or make a video of the damage and send us via email.

We will process your inquiry in the shortest possible time and contact you to clarify the warranty terms. You can request a refund or exchange if an item has a major problem. Otherwise we will repair the item within a reasonable time.

Please note: goods damaged during installation by the third party masters or a buyer, are not subject to exchange or return!

If the product you purchased from us does not meet your expectations or does not work properly, be sure to contact us via email first

Please note: We do not refund goods for those who are seeking a return due to a change of mind or if ordered providing the incorrect sizes, so be sure to choose carefully before purchasing so as to avoid this unfortunate occurrence.
