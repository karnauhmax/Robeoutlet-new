Payment and Delivery

To complete an order, we require a full payment to be organized over the web-site payment options.

Alternatively, please contact us directly, our team will send you the invoice, that can be paid via:

  • Direct bank transfer (find our bank details on the invoice)

  • AfterPay

  • PayID - aswshowers@gmail.com

  • PayPal

  • ZIPPa

You'll receive an order confirmation via email, once the payment is received on our side along with the payment receipt.

Depending on the product you purchased and the courier service will be used - the lead time can vary.

DELIVERY:

  • We are offering a flat delivery rate $60 per one order

  • We deal only with trusted courier services.

  • You'll receive a text message with the delivery date once your order has been processed.

  • On the delivery day you'll receive a call from the courier prior arrival.

  • Delivery is only available to the gate.

  • If the site unattended, we'll leave your order in the safe place.
